Privacy Policy for Mappy Tasks

Last updated: November 2025

Mappy Tasks (“the App”) is developed by an independent developer (“we,” “our,” or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Information

The App may access your device’s location only when you choose to enable location selection for a task. This information is used locally on your device to center the map view and help you pick a task location. We do not store, upload, or share your personal location at any time.

1.2 Photos / Camera

You may optionally attach photos to your tasks. If you use the camera, the App accesses it only to capture the photo you choose to save.

1.3 Task Data

The App stores your task information (such as name, description, date, reminder settings, and photo) using Firebase Firestore.

1.4 Analytics & Crash Reports

The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous usage data and crash information. This helps improve the App’s reliability and user experience. No personally identifiable information is collected.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data only for:

  • enabling core task management functions,
  • centering the map to help you pick a task location,
  • saving your tasks and attachments to the cloud,
  • diagnosing crashes and improving app performance.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Task-related data is securely stored in Firebase Firestore. Your photos are stored only if you attach them to a task.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics)

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
